Information on Maize & Blue Cupboard

At the University of Michigan we understand that college is expensive, that is way we have created the Maize & Blue cupboard. The Maize & Blue Cupboard acts as a food pantry for students who are having any type of food insecurity.


The Maize & Blue cupboard prioritizes privacy for students who might not want others to know they are using it You set up an appointment online and go in during the time you selected. The only information they need is a valid UofM ID to ensure that this is only for students.


The Maize & Blue cupboard is set up like a grocery store, you sign up for a 15 minute appointment slot and you are welcome to browse the selections. You can always go just to see what they have and leave with as much or as little as they need. Some high demand items do have a limit associated with them.

Additional Information

The Maize & Blue cupboard has more than just food. They have kitchen, personal, and household essentials as well as different recipes for the food you get.


If you urgently need food and can not find an available timeslot you are able to go to the Maize & Blue cupboard and they will provide you with a pre-packaged bag with various different types of food in it.
